# Branding & positioning — questions for Cale

Copy into an email or doc. Short bullets are fine.

## One-liners

1. **Elevator line:** In one sentence, how should strangers describe what you do (not your résumé — the *point*)?
2. **Promise:** What should a visitor *feel* or *know* after 30 seconds on your site?
3. **North star for the next 12 months:** Speaking bookings, podcast launch, institute growth, or community list — rank 1–4.

## Voice & tone

4. Three adjectives for your public voice (e.g. warm, unflinching, witty).
5. Words or phrases you want to **avoid** (overused, inaccurate, or not you)?
6. Humor: lean in, keep subtle, or mostly serious?

## Speaking

7. Ideal **formats** (parish mission, keynote, retreat, school, RCIA, men’s/women’s, etc.)?
8. Geographic reality: **Canada-first**, North America, or open to travel when it fits?
9. **Topics** you want to be known for in 3–6 bullet themes — and any topics to **not** list on the site.
10. Honorarium / process: “inquire,” rough range, or leave vague?

## Podcast (new show)

11. **Working title** (or “TBD” is fine) and **rough launch window** (quarter/year)?
12. **Format**: solo, co-host, interview-heavy, mixed?
13. **Guest pitch**: who is a *dream* guest — category is enough (author, bishop, athlete, etc.)?
14. **Audience**: same as speaking, or broader (seekers, cradle Catholics, young adults)?

## Faith Explained Institute

15. **Role**: “Founder” only, or also executive/editor — what title should the site use?
16. One sentence on what the Institute *is* for someone who’s never heard of it.
17. **Link**: one canonical URL when you’re ready (site, Linktree, etc.)?

## Past broadcast work

18. How much do you want **archives** mentioned — footnote only, one line, or remove entirely?
19. Any **must-say** credit or disclaimer for old shows; anything you’d rather not emphasize?

## Social proof

20. **Testimonials**: do you have 1–2 short quotes (with permission) or names of hosts/orgs to list later?
21. **Photos**: besides the blazer shot — any speaking-on-stage photo for a future “Speaking” page?

## Cardinal / patron

22. Exact **wording** you want next to Cardinal Collins (patron, friend of the Institute, etc.)?
23. Confirm you’re happy with **photo/crest** source and attribution as used.

## Visual & name

24. **URL**: stick with `caleclarke.com` only, or also `www` / subdomains later?
25. **Aesthetic**: more “quiet library,” “editorial magazine,” or “warm parish hall”?
26. Anything you **love** or **hate** on other speakers’ sites (examples optional)?
